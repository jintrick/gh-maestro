**OpenAI Cyber Policy Violation**: The Codex engine was blocked by OpenAI's safety policy. This can happen when:

- The prompt or context was flagged as potentially related to cybersecurity risks
- The OpenAI account has been temporarily restricted due to policy enforcement

This restriction is usually temporary. To resolve this:

1. Wait a few minutes and re-run the workflow
2. Check your [OpenAI usage policies](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/safety-best-practices) for more information
3. If the issue persists, review the workflow prompt for content that may be triggering the policy filter
